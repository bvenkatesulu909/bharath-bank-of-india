Privacy Policy

BBI Banking Demo — last updated 2026

BBI Banking Demo is an educational banking simulator. It is not a real bank, is not affiliated with the Reserve Bank of India or any financial institution, and holds no real money. Please use fictitious/sample data only.

1. Data we collect

None. This application does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts on any server, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking.

2. Data you enter

Any information you type into the app (name, email, sample profile details, demo transactions) is stored only on your own device using your browser's local storage (localStorage). It never leaves your device and is not sent to us or to any third party. You should enter sample data only — do not enter a real PAN, Aadhaar, bank details, or other real identity or financial information.

3. How to delete your data

Because all data is local, you can erase it at any time by logging out, clearing this app's / browser's site data, or uninstalling the app. There is no server-side copy to request deletion of.

4. Permissions

The app requires no special device permissions. It does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or files.

5. Children

The app is a general-audience educational demo and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.
